What is Propolis?

Propolis is a resinous substance that bees collect from tree buds, sap flows, and other botanical sources. They use it to seal and protect their hives, creating a safe and sterile environment for the colony. But propolis isn't just beneficial for bees; it also offers a wide range of advantages for us humans. Health Benefits of Propolis

    1. Immune System Support: Propolis is known for its immune-boosting properties. It contains various compounds, including flavonoids and antioxidants, that help strengthen our immune system and protect against common illnesses.

    3. Oral Health: Propolis has long been used in natural oral care products due to its antimicrobial properties. It can help fight against oral bacteria, prevent cavities, and promote overall oral health.
